Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

LaunchPe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use LaunchPe at launchpe.in.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials via Google Sign-In or email/password through Firebase Authentication.

Product URLs

When you paste a product URL into LaunchPe, we fetch and analyze that website's publicly accessible content to generate launch content. We do not store the full HTML — only the AI-generated results.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your card number, UPI ID, or banking details. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to all payment data.

Usage Data

We collect data on how you interact with the platform — pages visited, features used, content generated. This helps us improve the product.

Generated Content

We store the AI-generated posts and launch plans in your account so you can access them from your dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve LaunchPe's core functionality
  • To authenticate your account and manage your plan
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send account-related emails (payment confirmations, plan renewals)
  • To analyze aggregate usage and improve the product

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Ever.

3. Data Storage

Your account data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted on Google Cloud servers. Firebase is SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified. Data may be stored on servers located in the United States or other regions where Google Cloud operates.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and database
  • Razorpay — Payment processing
  • Groq AI — AI content generation (your product URL is sent to Groq's API for analysis)
  • Netlify — Hosting and serverless functions

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (Firebase session cookies). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your generated content from your dashboard
  • Opt out of any non-essential communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@launchpe.in

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Generated launch content may be retained in anonymised, aggregated form to improve AI quality.

8. Children's Privacy

LaunchPe is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you via email or a notice on the platform before changes take effect.
